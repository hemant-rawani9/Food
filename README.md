# 🍔 Food App (Android)

## 📱 Overview

**Food App** is a simple Android food ordering application developed using **Java** in **Android Studio**. The app provides a complete food ordering experience, starting from user registration to successful order placement. It is designed with a clean user interface and demonstrates the basic workflow of an online food delivery application.

# 📖 Application Workflow

### Step 1 - Splash Screen

When the application starts, the Splash Screen displays the app logo for a few seconds before navigating to the Login screen.

↓

### Step 2 - Register

New users can create an account by entering their basic details.

↓

### Step 3 - Login

Existing users log in using their registered credentials.

↓

### Step 4 - Dashboard / Home

After successful login, users reach the Home screen where they can browse different food categories.

Available categories:

* Pizza
* Burger
* Chinese
* South Indian
* Desserts
* Beverages

↓

### Step 5 - Select Food

Users browse food items and view available dishes.

↓

### Step 6 - Add to Wishlist

Users can save their favorite food items to the Wishlist for future orders.

↓

### Step 7 - Add to Cart

Selected food items are added to the Cart where users can review their order.

↓

### Step 8 - Checkout

Users proceed to the Checkout page to verify their order details.

↓

### Step 9 - Select Payment Method

Available payment methods include:

* Card Payment
* UPI
* Cash on Delivery (COD)

↓

### Step 10 - Order Confirmation

After successful payment or COD confirmation, the app displays an **Order Successful** message.

↓

### Step 11 - Order History

The placed order is stored in the Order History section for future reference.

↓

### Step 12 - Profile

Users can view and update their personal information.

↓

### Step 13 - Settings

Users can access the Settings page to manage application preferences.
